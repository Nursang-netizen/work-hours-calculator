# Work Hours Calculator

A free, mobile-friendly tool to calculate your work hours and track earnings. Perfect for freelancers, hourly workers, and anyone who wants to keep track of their shift payments.

## Features

- **Easy Calculation**: Input your clock-in/out times, break duration, and hourly rate
- **Date Range Support**: Calculate earnings for multiple days at once
- **Shift History**: Keep a record of all your shifts with editable entries
- **Export Data**: Copy your shift records to clipboard in CSV format
- **Mobile Friendly**: Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops
- **No Sign-up Required**: Everything runs in your browser, no account needed
- **Offline Support**: Data is saved locally on your device

## How to Use

1. **Set Your Schedule**: Enter your clock-in and clock-out times
2. **Add Break Time**: Enter any break duration in minutes
3. **Set Hourly Rate**: Enter your pay rate per hour
4. **Select Date Range**: Choose start and end dates for calculation
5. **Calculate**: Click "Calculate Total Work Hours" to see your earnings
6. **Track History**: All calculations are saved automatically
